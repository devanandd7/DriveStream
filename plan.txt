1. What Data We Collect
We collect only what is essential for the service to function:

Google Account ID & Name (for authentication)
File Metadata (names, sizes, types, and folder structures)
Encrypted Access Tokens (stored securely to communicate with Drive)
Telegram User ID (to link your account to the remote control bot)
Playback Progress (to sync where you left off between devices)
Note: We do NOT collect passwords, personal email content, or the actual content of your files.
2. Why We Use Your Data
Your data is used exclusively to provide the core DriveStream Pro experience:

To build your personal media library interface.
To allow instant searching via the Telegram bot.
To sync your viewing history across web and mobile.
To ensure only you can access your private files.
We Never Sell Your Data
